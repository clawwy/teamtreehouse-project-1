A Story About Silver

Hi, my name is Clara, or you can call me Silver.

I am a freshly graduated dietitian from Malaysia. Currently, I am working in pharmacy as dietitian cum product trainer.

I love art and music very much. I would say, more than my life. I spent all my time after work in drawing, writing calligraphy, singing, playing guitar or piano, and writing. Sometimes, I make videos too.

Signing up for a web development course is something that wasn't plan in my life. I learnt basic html when I was twelve, and I used to do minimal editing on my blog html/css file previously. I found that web design is fun. I love to see beautiful website, and that's why I'm here - to apply my skills and creativity in designing beautiful websites, for me and for others, in future.
